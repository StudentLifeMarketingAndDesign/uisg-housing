SAFE RIDE CAB SERVICE

The University of Iowa Student Government (UISG) has paired up with a local cab service to provide FREE and safe transportation for all University of Iowa Students. This cab service is meant to offer UI students emergency transport home to distance themselves from potentially dangerous situations.

How it works:

If you’re downtown and find yourself in an emergency situation, just pick up your phone and call Safe Ride. They’ll bring you from your current location to your home for absolutely no cost to you.

(Emergency) Numbers:

Safe Ride Cab Service: 319-367-HOME(4663)
Nite Ride: 319-384-1111
*Nite Ride is run by the UI Dept. of Public Safety: http://police.uiowa.edu/services/nite-ride/

Iowa City Non-Emergency Police: 319-356-5275
In case of an emergency, always call 911.
